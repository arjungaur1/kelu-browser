# Privacy Policy for K.E.L.U. Browser

**Effective Date:** May 30, 2026

Welcome to K.E.L.U. Browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
K.E.L.U. is designed with a "Privacy-First" architecture. We strictly limit the data we collect:
* **Voice Data:** K.E.L.U. operates using a voice-first interface. To provide zero-latency and maximum privacy, our core voice-to-DOM processing engine runs **offline** directly on your device. We do not store your voice recordings on our servers.
* **App Usage Data:** We may collect non-personal, anonymous crash reports and basic usage metrics to improve the app's stability and performance.

## 2. Third-Party AI Services
K.E.L.U. offers a customizable mode allowing users to plug in their preferred third-party AI APIs (such as Gemini, Claude, etc.). 
* If you choose to integrate an external API, your text/voice queries processed through that specific API are subject to the privacy policy of the respective API provider. 
* We do not intercept, store, or sell any data transmitted between your device and your chosen AI provider.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We believe that user data belongs to the user. 
* **Local Processing:** The majority of your browsing data and core AI operations are processed locally on your hardware. 
* We do not maintain databases of your browsing history, bookmarks, or personal preferences on external servers.

## 4. Permissions Required
To function effectively, K.E.L.U. requires the following standard device permissions:
* **Microphone Access:** Strictly to receive your voice commands for web navigation.
* **Internet Access:** To browse websites and connect to third-party APIs if configured by the user.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy as we roll out new features. Any changes will be updated on this page with a revised "Effective Date". We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
